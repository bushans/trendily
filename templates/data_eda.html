Data and EDA Examples

Data

Training data came from the DeepFashion Dataset - Category and Attribute Predictions. This dataset included 289,222 images of clothing, tagged with one of 50 categories, and any of 1000 Attribute tags. After looking at the data, I reduced the categories to 33 and discarded images to decrease a huge class imbalance amongst categories. After cleaning and data transformations (flips, zooms, etc), the category CNN ended up training about 153,000 images for 33 categories. An example of the images with their category labels can be seen below.


There were 5 different Attribute groups, of which I used 4 (Fabric, Texture, Shape, Parts). Each image was potentially tagged with multiple attributes, so this was a multi-classification problem. Examples of the data tags can be seen below.
