Annoy

I utilized Spotify's library, Annoy (Approximate Nearest Neighbors Oh Yeah), to create an embeddings index and to generate recommendations. Annoy is built on the assumption that speed is more important than perfect recommendations. The library allows you to select your speed/accuracy tradeoff threshold, and will find the approximately nearest neighbors from the embeddings index to the input image, based on the distance metric selected. In this case, recommendations were based on the cosine similarity of image embeddings.
