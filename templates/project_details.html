Trendily - Women's Fashion Recommender

Introduction

I often find myself looking at other women's outfits and thinking to myself, I wonder where she got that {article of clothing} from! This is particularly true when scrolling through social media, where people tend to post their best-looking photos. To solve this problem, I created a women's fashion recommender that could take a photo (say, from a social media outlet), analyze it, and recommend similar items that a user might like to try. This recommender system eases the debate I have all too often: awkwardly ask her where she buys her clothes, or spend the next 2 months searching stores and the internet for a similar piece.

I've created a recommendation system that, based on an image selected by the user, can recommend similar options available on Rent the Runway for the user to try. To create this recommender, I trained 5 Convolutional Neural Networks to perform specific tasks, such as classifying the type of clothing (pants/shirt/jacket) or classifying clothing attributes (stripes, floral, lace, etc)). After training the CNNs, I concatenated the trained embeddings from each model to create a complex "description" of each photo. Recommendations are based on cosine similarity of the inventory embeddings to the embedding of the user-input photo
